Key verdict: 97% of the decline is within-channel, not mix shift

Initial hypothesis that autobooking channel surge caused the decline was disproved. Counterfactual analysis shows channel mix shift explains only 0.13pp of the 3.38pp decline. The real drivers are: (1) Math Tutoring H2A collapsing while becoming 48% of volume, (2) The Coding Knight targeting wrong age groups, and (3) systemic lead quality erosion across all channels. This is a product and scaling problem — not a manager, channel, or BM problem.
