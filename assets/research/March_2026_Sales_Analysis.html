Ramadan or Structural? — The Answer

Ramadan 2026 began ~1 March. Some decline is expected. But is it the primary driver?
If Ramadan were primary driver, we'd expect…
✗Lead volume to drop — people less active
✗A2S to fall — lower purchase intent
✗B2A to fall sharply — less willingness to attend
✗Drop uniform across all channels
✗Automatic recovery in April (Shawwal)

✓A2S STABLE: 31.8% → 32.3% (slightly UP)
✓B2A mild drop: 39.9% → 36.2% — modest
✓Drop concentrated in Google channel specifically
✓BM attrition visible in individual CRM records
Verdict: ~80% Structural · ~20% Seasonal
Ramadan may explain a mild B2A dip (families less responsive to scheduling) and possibly some BM absenteeism. But lead demand held up, purchase intent held up, and TCM performance held up. Fixing the structural issues will recover the majority of lost revenue regardless of whether Ramadan is over.

Proposed Metric

Lead Quality Score — One Number for Month-over-Month Tracking

The Problem with Raw L2B%
L2B is falling — but why? Because the channel mix shifted (Google replacing Facebook), or because leads within channels got worse (spam, low-intent)? Raw L2B cannot tell you. You need a metric that separates these two effects.
Formula — Lead Quality Score (LQS)
LQS = Actual L2B% ÷ Expected L2B% × 100

Expected L2B% =
  Σ ( channeli_share × channeli_baseline )
LQS = 100 → leads convert exactly as expected for the current channel mix
LQS > 100 → leads outperform expectations (strong in-channel targeting)
LQS < 100 → leads underperform expectations — spam, low-intent, broad targeting

Action Plan

What To Fix — Prioritised Actions

URGENT
Rebalance channel mix — reduce Google Ads budget immediately
Set Google CPL hard cap. Shift incremental budget back to Facebook. Only run Google with purchase-optimized conversion objectives — not broad traffic.
URGENT
Rebuild BM headcount to 30+ active (currently 25)
5 inactive BMs = ~3,500 lost bookings/month at peak. Fast-track hiring or reactivate standby BMs. Each active BM recovers ~700 bookings/month.
MEDIUM
Add lead quality filter before BM assignment
Auto-tag suspected spam, invalid, and low-intent leads at entry. Route to auto-nurture sequence — not live BM calls. Protect BM utilisation.
MEDIUM
Restore TCM capacity to 160+ active (currently 144)
Investigate March departures. Re-activate idle TCMs. At 17 MKs/TCM, utilisation is unsustainably low — target 25+ MKs/TCM.
MONITOR
Track Ramadan recovery in April — validate structural fix
If structural fixes are applied and A2S stays stable, revenue should recover in April. If L2B remains depressed after Ramadan, the channel mix issue is not yet resolved.
