Proposed Metric

Lead Quality Score (LQS) — Month-over-Month Quality Tracking

The Problem with Raw L2B%

L2B is falling — but why? Because the channel mix shifted (Google replacing Facebook), or because leads within channels got worse (spam, low-intent)? Raw L2B cannot answer this. You need a metric that separates these two effects cleanly.

Formula — Lead Quality Score (LQS)
LQS = Actual L2B% ÷ Expected L2B% × 100

Expected L2B% =
  Σ( channeli_share × channeli_baseline )
LQS = 100 → leads convert exactly as expected for the channel mix
LQS > 100 → leads outperform — strong in-channel targeting
LQS < 100 → leads underperform — spam, low-intent, broad targeting
